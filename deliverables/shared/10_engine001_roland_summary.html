Quick Reminder — What Mango Lab Is

Mango Lab is the fully automated ads meter tool. Connect your ad accounts and the platform measures, monitors, and surfaces what's actually working — without you having to open Meta Ads Manager, Google Ads, or a spreadsheet ever again.

It is the product we are building, the brand whose voice you've already approved (industrial brutalism, three colours, Berlin), and the project this class budget is being requested for.

1 · The Strategy Funnel

Five stages, instrumented end to end. Every drop-off is measurable, which is the entire point — the campaign teaches us where the funnel is leaking, which is the same thing the Mango Lab tool does for our future users.

Targets: 15–30 sign-ups, 50%+ activation, and a cost per sign-up below €25. These numbers are conservative against published Meta benchmarks for B2B SaaS in DACH.

2 · Why This Strategy (Three Reasons)

01
The campaign is the demo
Mango Lab measures ads. So we are running our own ads — and the meter is measuring them in real time. Anyone who clicks through sees the product doing the thing the product claims to do. There is no gap between message and proof.
02
14 days is the right window
Meta's algorithm needs ~50 conversions per week to escape its learning phase. At €28.50/day across 14 days we hit that threshold. A shorter burst wastes 70–80% on un-optimised delivery; a longer thin spread never converges. This is the smallest credible test.
03
Every euro is instrumented
No agency margin, no production cost, no tooling fees — creatives are AI-generated, the funnel is wired with free-tier tools. Every cent of the €400 buys an impression we can attribute. This is precisely the discipline the class is meant to teach.

# A note on the two extras I added

The standard ask was five artifacts, I added two more because honestly the document on its own felt kind of incomplete to me. The main paper has all the moves in it, but a lot of the reasoning behind why we did what we did kind of gets lost in the prose, and I wanted to fix that.

## The Framework Application Map (Artifact 6)

A single page where the four frameworks that shaped Mango Lab sit next to the actual moves they shaped. Why we charged €49 instead of giving the trial away for free. Why Bet & Babes was our first client and not someone we cold-pitched. Why we ran paid META ads before there was even a product to sell. Lean Startup, Disciplined Entrepreneurship, Y Combinator, and the Mom Test. Theory on one side of each panel, the actual move on the other, so the connection is visible.

## The Mango Lab Growth Model (Artifact 7)

Most decks I have read kind of skip the part where they walk you through how the company actually grows from one stage to the next, and I wanted that part written down properly. Four stages, each with a trigger that unlocks the next one. The flywheel that connects them. The unit economics that prove the math is not delusional.

Both extras came from the same place, basically. I did not want this document to be a list of frameworks I read about in books. I wanted it to be a record of how those frameworks actually shaped a company that exists, with real clients, real numbers, and real people running it.

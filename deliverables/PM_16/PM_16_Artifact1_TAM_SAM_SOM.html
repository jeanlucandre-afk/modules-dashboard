Methodology

TAM Top-down

Global SMB digital advertising spend tracked by Technavio at $356B for 2024, growing at ~11% CAGR. Includes paid social, search, and display across all SMB segments worldwide — the broadest possible pool of budget that a tool like Mango Lab competes for attention within.

SAM Serviceable market

The AI-powered marketing automation and creative tools segment, specifically targeting SMBs running paid social campaigns. Narrowed from TAM by focusing on software spend (not ad spend itself) and on SMB segments with fewer than 50 employees operating in English and Spanish-speaking markets.

SOM Bottom-up (yr 3)

Conservative scenario built from the current pricing model: 160,000 subscribers at an average of $250/year ARPU = $40M ARR, representing 0.1% SAM capture. Grounded in observed trial conversion data from the LinkedIn outreach sprint and the €40/month entry price point.

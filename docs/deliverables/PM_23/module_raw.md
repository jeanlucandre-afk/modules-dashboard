## Module Description

Important — this module is a 10 ECTS combination of two separate 5 ECTS modules which are being phased out: PM04 Marketing and PM17 Sales

### Knowledge Areas

Students who successfully pass this module are able to:
- Understand the essential concepts of marketing and sales, growth marketing and the customer lifecycle
- Understand consumer behavior as it relates to product marketing initiatives and marketing strategies for B2B and B2C sales processes
- Apply online marketing instruments, including SEO, SEA, social media marketing, and affiliate marketing, to create effective marketing campaigns
- Analyze key performance indicators (KPIs) to control the success of digital marketing campaigns, conduct A/B tests, and use statistical tools to ensure quality of results
- Create effective sales techniques to negotiate and close deals and managing sales funnels
- Create exceptional customer experiences, drive brand loyalty and enable viral growth

### Learning Resources & Disclaimer

The scope of the subject matter of this single module is enormous. Learning units offered in relation to this module do not cover the full breadth of topics in this module. ## Standard Assessment Instructions

Students will apply topics covered in this module in their projects and demonstrate the resulting learning progress during the semester. This demonstration of learning progress is structured in the following points, all of which are mandatory:

1. **One module-specific check-in session** during the project phase where students present how they applied the methods and tools in the project and their effects on overall product development. They can be booked with Roland.

2. **Active participation** in consulting sessions with PM faculty and potentially faculty from other study programs.

3. **By the announced deadline, two written reports**, one being a Marketing specific report, and the second being a Sales specific report:

   **The Marketing Report** (about 18 pages incl. charts/images) where the student reflects about the results and potential next steps regarding methods and tools to be applied in the project. All written text must fulfill standards of academic writing, including correct language and consistent citation style (APA). Failing to include citations for references in the text means the module cannot be passed. The examples and reflection must be handed in as a single PDF document.

   **Sales Report — enablement deck/presentation** exemplifying the development of the product sales strategy (own project or analyzed external company/product portfolio) on the basis of the methods covered in this module: including an analysis of the user and buyer persona, value-based product proposition, a cold outreach template or an objection handling template, an example sales funnel with qualified leads.

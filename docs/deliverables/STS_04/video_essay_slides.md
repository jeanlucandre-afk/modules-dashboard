# STS_04: Video Essay Slides

> Slide deck to accompany the 10-min video essay. Each slide is timed to a beat in `video_essay_script.md`. Designed for a 16:9 frame, dark background, single sans-serif (Inter or similar), generous margins. Slides should be on screen as cards or lower-thirds, never crowding the speaker.

**Total slides:** 18
**Aspect:** 16:9
**Suggested style:** Black background, white text, one accent colour (#FF6B35 or similar warm) for emphasis. Quotes in a slightly smaller serif (e.g. Source Serif) to mark them as voices other than mine.

## Production notes

- Quote cards (3, 4, 5, 7, 9, 10, 14, 15, 18): serif, italic optional, attribution in smaller weight underneath.
- My-voice cards (1, 12, 16, 17): sans-serif, no quote marks, accent colour permitted on key word.
- Concept / build slides (6, 8, 11, 13): keep one font, vary size for hierarchy.
- All slides exported as 1920x1080 PNG and dropped on the timeline at the timestamps above. Hold each card for the duration of the line it supports, then cut back to camera.
- Total on-screen card time should be roughly 4 to 4.5 minutes of the 10. The other 5.5 to 6 minutes is me on camera with no graphic.

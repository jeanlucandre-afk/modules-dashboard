SECTION_07 / ACTIVATION

Activation — Sign-up to First Insight

Activation is the real conversion. A sign-up that doesn't connect an ad account is a bounce in disguise. The activation flow has to compress the path from email-submitted to first-insight-delivered into 60 seconds.

Step 1 — Immediate (Post-form-submit)

User lands on the OAuth screen by default. No "welcome page," no "tour," no "verify your email first." The single visible CTA: "Connect Meta Ads (read-only)." A small footer line: "We never write to your account. SOC 2 in progress. GDPR-native."

Step 2 — Post-OAuth (Within 60 sec)

Meter pulls 12 months of historical performance from the connected ad account. Dashboard renders with: spend trend, top-performing creative, the single biggest week-over-week change, and one "anomaly card" highlighting something the user probably didn't know. The anomaly card is the activation moment. It's the proof.

Step 3 — Activation Reminder (For users who sign up but don't connect)

+2h email: "You signed up but the meter is empty. Connect a Meta account here — takes 30 seconds, read-only. → mango-lab.de/connect" +24h email: "Quick reminder. The dashboard is waiting. Connect here, see the first anomaly inside a minute. → mango-lab.de/connect" +72h: stop. Don't be that SaaS.
The meter has to surprise the user inside their first session. If the first anomaly card is generic ("CPM increased 12% week-over-week"), the user thinks "I knew that." If it's specific ("Your highest-CTR creative had its frequency capped on day 9 — costing you ~€420 in missed conversions"), the user thinks "Holy shit." That's the difference between a sign-up and an activated user.

SECTION_08 / QA

Brand Compliance Checklist

Every creative ships through this gate before it goes live in Meta Ads Manager. If any answer is "no," the creative gets sent back.

CheckPass criterion
Color paletteOnly #FC5816, #000000, #EFEED6 visible. No navy. No tints, no gradients, no transparency blends. No pure white anywhere.
Logo treatmentReads as stamped — visible ink bleed, analog noise. Never a clean digital render. Mascot never separated from wordmark in the official lockup.
Mascot scaleCorner element only, <15% canvas. Never centered, never the dominant element, never recolored, never outlined.
TypographyDisplay = Monument Extended Ultrabold (Archivo Black web fallback). Technical = JetBrains Mono Light. Body = DejaVu Sans Bold (Inter 700 fallback). No mixing of weights or roles.
TrackingDisplay: +60 (0.06em). Mono: +180 (0.18em). Body: +80 (0.08em). All caps on display + technical layers.
Voice axisCreative reads as either HUMAN or TECHNICAL — not both, not neither. If it's "kind of both," it's neither.
Stamp aestheticWhole composition feels printed, not screened. Letterpress-quality ink imperfection on type.
CTA terminologyButton copy is "Get the Meter" / "Connect Now" / "See the Dashboard" — never "Learn More," never "Sign Up Today," never "Schedule a Demo."
